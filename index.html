About Us

A decade of steady, honest teaching

Saba Tuition Center started in a single rented room with 6 students. Today we run structured batches across Primary, Middle, Matric, O/A-Level and Intermediate levels — with the same promise: every child is tracked, not just taught.

Mission

No Child Left Behind

Every student gets individual attention regardless of batch size — that's non-negotiable for our teachers.

Method

Concept Before Cramming

We teach the "why" behind formulas and rules first, so exam prep becomes revision, not memorization.

Promise

Fee Transparency

What you see on the class card is what you pay — multi-subject discounts are applied automatically, nothing hidden.
